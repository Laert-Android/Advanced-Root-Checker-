Advanced Root Checker detects root indicators on your 
Android device. All 15 checks run entirely offline.
No internet connection required, no data sent anywhere.

WHAT IT DETECTS:
- su binaries in common and hidden paths
- Magisk and Magisk modules
- SuperSU and SuperUser APKs
- BusyBox binary
- Xposed Framework
- Known root manager apps
- Dangerous system properties
- Read-write /system mount
- Writable /system partition
- SELinux permissive mode
- Test-keys build signature
- Suspicious build fingerprint
- su execution test

PRIVACY:
- No internet permission
- No ads or ad SDKs
- No analytics or tracking
- No data collection of any kind

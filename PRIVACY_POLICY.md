# Privacy Policy for Advanced Root Checker

Last updated: May 2025

## No Data Collection
Advanced Root Checker does not collect, store, or 
transmit any personal data. All checks run entirely 
on your device.

## No Internet Access
The app has no internet permission and cannot 
communicate with any server.

## No Ads
The app contains no advertisements or ad SDKs.

## No Tracking
The app contains no analytics, crash reporting, 
or tracking of any kind.

## No Third Party Libraries
The app uses no external libraries or SDKs.

## Open Source
The full source code is available at:
https://github.com/Laert-Android/Advanced-Root-Checker-

## Contact
If you have any questions open an issue on GitHub.
